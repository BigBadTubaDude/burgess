Welcome!

Thank you for visiting my website. I am excited about running to become the next Superintendent of Education for the State of South Carolina.

It is my vision to promote academic excellence, community-local control of our public schools, and educator-teacher pride. I am in this fight as a South Carolinian to ensure that the state we all love and cherish upholds its most fundamental charge to society-
the education of our youth to work toward forming a more perfect union.

South Carolina will set the stage for the continuance of the America that we admire. I am asking you to join me in this campaign to make our public school system the best there is, world-class, allowing our students and citizens to compete with anyone, anywhere in this country and world. All children are our children.

How can you help? By making a donation to the campaign and by voting for me. Again, thanks for visiting the website.
